Waterford Aerial Photography is based in the South East of Ireland and run by the same family for the last 10 years.

We have a passion for photography and flying and we think when the two are combined the results can be spectacular.

In the past we have worked on Weddings, Circus, Concerts, Racing, Parades and many other events. We are always looking for that new challenge and are very willing to work on proof of concepts and experimental ideas.

We have 2 pilots and use a range of camera equipment and drones from DJI. We are fully licenced and insured.
